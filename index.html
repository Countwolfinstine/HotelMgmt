About The Generic Restaurant

Our 4 star hotel in Bangalore opened its doors in September 2003 and it’s menu is carefully created so that it caters to a wide audience. Starting with authentic Indian food to a mixing it with some influence from other cultures we make sure our menu’s are up to date to the tastes of our customers. Located in the middle of the city our customers enjoy quick access to most of the facilities that the city provides.

At The Triton hotel, Chef Vikas Mehta, Bangalore’s Best Chef (Bangalore Magazine) and one of India’s Best New Chefs (Food), combines his trademark “refined rusticity” with an unyielding passion for the best local, seasonal, and organic ingredients. Our menu and tasting menus change, so you can be sure our ingredients are the very best of the best that day.

Whether you join us for a special night out, unmistakably “Generic” private party, our famous Chef’s Whim – or a Chef’s Tasting Menu (with a ringside view of your meal being prepared! ) – when you eat out at The Generic Restaurant, you will feel right at home.
